How to Play

KidsMath is simple to play and a fun way to learn basic math skills. All you have to do is enter a username in the box below, then choose a level of difficulty and how much time you have to answer each question. Hit the "Submit" button and the page will bring up a series of 10 equations for you to solve.That's it!
